The loop

One team, one data thread — sky to soil.

We fly multispectral missions over farms, mine concessions and industrial sites to map crop stress, vegetation health and environmental compliance risk. The organic waste streams from those same operations feed our Black Soldier Fly colonies — producing protein-rich larvae meal and frass fertilizer that return, informed by the very maps we just flew, to the soil that needs them most.

01 — Aerial intelligence

Multispectral drone monitoring

The DJI Mavic 3 Multispectral captures RGB plus four discrete narrow-band sensors — green, red, red edge and near-infrared — alongside an RTK module for survey-grade positioning. Every flight produces a stack of geo-referenced outputs ready for analysis.

RGB

Visible light — true-colour base map

Green — 560nm

Canopy reflectance, chlorophyll content

Red — 650nm

Chlorophyll absorption, plant stress

Red edge — 730nm

Early stress detection, nitrogen status

NIR — 840nm

Biomass, vegetation vigour

02 — Circular bioconversion

Black Soldier Fly systems

Hermetia illucens larvae convert organic waste streams — market refuse, brewery spent grain, abattoir by-products — into two high-value outputs in roughly two weeks: protein-rich larvae meal for animal feed, and nutrient-dense frass for soil amendment.

Larvae meal

40–45% crude protein, lauric-acid rich — a fishmeal and soymeal substitute for poultry, fish and pig feed formulations.

Frass fertilizer

Chitin-rich organic soil amendment that improves structure, microbial activity and nutrient availability — targeted using drone-mapped deficiency zones.

Waste intake

Market organics, brewery spent grain and agro-processing residues diverted from landfill and converted within 24–48 hours of collection.

03 — Regulatory & institutional

Environmental compliance monitoring

Backed by OHS certification and licensed drone operation, we deliver monitoring datasets formatted for EIA submissions, NEMC reporting and ongoing compliance audits — air quality, noise, vibration and water quality, cross-referenced with aerial imagery.

Air quality

PM2.5 / PM10 dust dispersion mapping around mining, quarrying and construction sites, cross-referenced with wind data.

Noise & vibration

Perimeter sound-level surveys and blast vibration monitoring for industrial and extraction operations near communities.

Water quality

Surface water sampling and turbidity assessment near tailings, runoff zones and downstream community intake points.

EIA & audit reporting

Survey data packaged into NEMC-aligned reports, repeat-flight baselines and third-party verification records.
